After graduating, I moved to Portland with the intention of attending law school in 2018. After working as a legal assistant for a few months, I abandoned these ambitions and enrolled at Epicodus in hopes of pursuing a career in web development.

Studying sociology has helped me establish a passion for advocacy, justice, and activism. I hope to eventually merge this background with my interest in coding by supporting nonprofit organizations and community interests.
